Ongoing hug — -고 있어

안+고 있어

안고 있어 = I'm hugging · -고 있어 ≈ “am …ing”

Two words for “hug”

포옹
noun · a hug
안다
verb · to hug

꽉 = tightly · 널 = you (object, casual)

See — 보다

보다 = to see · to watch · to look (at something)

Basic verb from class — same idea as English “see / look / watch”.

Not great — 별로네

별로네

“Not great.” / “Meh.” / “Nothing special.”

별로 = not really · not particularly · -네 = “I see / so that's how it is”

I'm back & welcome back — ただいま / おかえり

From chat with Yoon (14 Jul) — Korean homecoming phrases paired with Japanese.

ただいま → 다녀왔어
I'm back · casual
다녀왔습니다
I'm back · formal
おかえり → 다녀왔어?
Welcome back? · casual
다녀오셨습니까
Welcome back? · formal

다녀오다 = to go out and come back · statement when you arrive · question when welcoming someone else.
