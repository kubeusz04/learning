Hugs · 안고 있어 · 안아줄게

🤗 1 · Two words for “hug”

KoreanTypeEnglishPolish
포옹nouna hugprzytulenie
안다verbto hugprzytulać

From class with Yoon — same topic as Polish Lesson 3 with Kuba.

🫂 2 · I'm hugging — 안고 있어

-고 있어 shows an action in progress — like English “am …ing”.

I'm hugging. 안고 있어 Przytulam cię. (right now)
안+고 있어

💙 3 · I'll hug you — 안아줄게

Casual promise to hug someone — often translated as “I'll hug you.”

I'll hug you. 안아줄게 Przytulę cię.

안아주다 = to hug (for someone) · -줄게 = I'll do (for you)

💕 4 · Full sentence from chat

나는 널 꽉 안고 있어 = I'm hugging you tight.
WordMeaning
나는I (topic)
널you — object form of 너 (casual)
꽉tightly / firmly
안고 있어am hugging
Polish pair Przytulam cię bardzo mocno.

👀 5 · See — 보다

Basic verb from class: see · watch · look at something.

see / watch / look 보다

😐 6 · Not great — 별로네

Casual reaction when something isn't impressive — “meh”, “not great”.

별로네 = Not great. / Nothing special. / Meh.

별로 = not really · -네 = ending showing you just noticed / realized something.

🏠 7 · I'm back & welcome back

From chat with Yoon (14 Jul) — Korean paired with Japanese homecoming phrases.
