🫂 2 · I'm hugging — 안고 있어

-고 있어 shows an action in progress — like English “am …ing”.

I'm hugging. 안고 있어 Przytulam cię. (right now)
안+고 있어

💙 3 · I'll hug you — 안아줄게

Casual promise to hug someone — often translated as “I'll hug you.”

I'll hug you. 안아줄게 Przytulę cię.

안아주다 = to hug (for someone) · -줄게 = I'll do (for you)

💕 4 · Full sentence from chat

나는 널 꽉 안고 있어 = I'm hugging you tight.
WordMeaning
나는I (topic)
널you — object form of 너 (casual)
꽉tightly / firmly
안고 있어am hugging
Polish pair Przytulam cię bardzo mocno.
