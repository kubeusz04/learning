ten, ta, to & tego

This / that by gender — and why tego shows up

Topic: Demonstratives · gender · genitive tego

1 · ten / ta / to — pick by gender

English just says “this / that”. Polish picks a form that matches the noun’s gender.

ten
masculine · he / this (m.)
ta
feminine · she / this (f.)
to
neuter · it / this (n.)
  • Ten chłopak · ten kwiat · ten dzień — masculine nouns
  • Ta dziewczyna · ta książka · ta noc — feminine nouns
  • To dziecko · to miasto · to niebo — neuter nouns
Quick test Ask: does the noun feel like on (he), ona (she), or ono (it)? → use ten / ta / to.

2 · Extra jobs for to

to is not only “this + neuter noun”. It also points at a whole thing or introduces something.

  • To jest kot. = This is a cat. / It’s a cat.
  • Co to? = What’s that?
  • To miłość. = This is love. / It’s love.
  • Lubię to. = I like this / that (the thing we’re talking about).

When there is no noun after it (or after jest), English “this / that / it” is often just to.

3 · Why tego? — genitive of ten & to

tego is not a fourth gender. It’s another case of ten or to — the genitive (“of this / of that”).

ten → tego
masculine genitive
to → tego
neuter genitive (same form!)

When Polish wants genitive, you get tego

  • After negation of “have / there is”: Nie ma tego. = There isn’t that. / That isn’t here.
  • After “I don’t like”: Nie lubię tego. = I don’t like this.
  • After “I’m afraid of”: Boję się tego. = I’m afraid of that.
  • With a masculine noun: Nie lubię tego filmu. = I don’t like this film.
  • With a neuter noun: Nie rozumiem tego słowa. = I don’t understand this word.
  • After some prepositions: do tego · bez tego · od tego · dla tego

Rule of thumb: if English has “of this / of that”, or Polish used a genitive verb/preposition — check if you need tego.

4 · Feminine is different — tej / tę

Don’t force tego onto feminine nouns. Feminine forms change differently.

ta
nominative
tej
genitive · of this (f.)
tę
accusative · this (f.)
tego
only m. / n.
  • Nie lubię tej książki. — not tego książki
  • Widzę tę dziewczynę. — accusative after “I see”
  • Nie ma tej nocy. — genitive after nie ma

Masculine animate object often also takes tego: Lubię tego chłopaka. = I like this guy.

6 · Love sentence

💕 Jestem najszczęśliwszy na świecie, że cię mam!

I’m the happiest in the world that I have you!

najszczęśliwszy = masculine · woman says najszczęśliwsza · comma before że is normal in Polish.
